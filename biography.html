Anna M. Ritcey

Full professor
Department of Chemistry
Université Laval

Education

B.Sc. Honours Chemistry, Acadia University, Nova Scotia (1978-1982)
Ph.D. Chemistry, McGill University, Montréal (1982-1988)
Thesis : Chiroptical Properties of Cellulose Derivatives (Supervisor : Derek Gray)
Postdoctoral fellow, Max-Planck Institute for Polymer Research, Mainz, Germany (1988-1989)
Postdoctoral fellow, Chemistry, Université Laval (1989-1991)

Professeur étoile, Faculté des sciences et de génie,Université Laval : 2001, 2004, 2009, 2010, 2016
Women's Faculty Award, Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (1991-1996)
Alexander von Humboldt Research Fellowship, West Germany (1988-1989)
1967 Science Scholarship, Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (1982-1986)
D.W. Ambridge Award for best Ph.D. thesis in physical sciences, McGill University (1988)
Winkler Award for best Ph.D. thesis in Chemistry, McGill University (1988)
Pulp and Paper Research Institute of Canada Presidential Citation for distinguished achievement in the Institute program at McGill (1988)
Governor General's Medal, Acadia University (1982)
Bronze Medal in Chemistry, Acadia University (1982)
